Inception is a 2010 Action & Sci-fi Film that is directed by Christopher Nolan, Cinematography by Wally Pfister, Produced by Christopher Nolan, Emma Thomas, Composed by Hans Zimmer and screenplay written by Christopher Nolan.

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Elliot Page, Marion Cotillard, Ken Watanabe, Tom Hardy, Cillian Murphy, Tom Berenger, Dileep Rao, Lukas Nash, Pete Postlethwaite, and Michael Caine.

The film released on July 22nd 2010 in theatres and grossed over $837 million worldwide. The film has a runtime of 148 minutes, or 2 hours and 28 minutes; and had a budget of $160 million.

A thief who steals corporate secrets through the use of dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a C.E.O., but his tragic past may doom the project and his team to disaster.
